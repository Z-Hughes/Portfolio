Being accepted into the engineering program at University of South Florida was one of the most exciting days of my life. I’ve always been someone who has both a creative and a logical side, so when I discovered software development and web design, I realized it would be the perfect fit. In school, my focus was dominantly on back end development, logic and problem solving. In my recent work, I have been focusing more on front-end development and design. This allows me to use my logical side to code, while also nourishing my creative side with design. I love not only being able to see the results, but the full life cycle and development process of an application.

After a very fulfilling career in hospitality and consulting, I decided to follow my true passion and begin a new journey into software development. After my first degree, I never expected to return to a university, but once I began my path in computer science, I immediately knew I had made the right decision. Changing careers later in life is never an easy thing to do. However, I’m not one to shy away from a challenge, and nothing was going to hold me back from making my dream a reality. It has taken a lot of work, and many long nights, but I made it. Now I’m here and ready to build the future one application at a time. However, my goal isn’t just to build your next website or application. My goal is to build your business.
